🛡️ True Serverless

We don't own your data. Nadirlon connects through "dumb" WebSocket Relays. It broadcasts encrypted noise. Your phone holds the only keys capable of reversing that noise.

🔒 NIP-44 Deep Crypto

Every Direct Message uses ChaCha20-Poly1305 authenticated encryption over an X25519 Diffie-Hellman key exchange. Interceptors only see random blobs.

👻 Nameless Avatars

Forget phone numbers. When you launch Nadirlon, a localized cryptographic identity (npub) is minted exclusively on your hardware. Claim any nickname you want.

🌍 Community Sovereignty

Don't trust public relays? Good. Fork the app, set EXPO_PUBLIC_DEFAULT_RELAY, and point it to your own private Node.js Mini-Relay. It takes 10 minutes to grant your community total self-governance.

🧅 Tor-Network Ready

Delegated OS-level proxying. Download Orbot, enable VPN Mode, and all Nadirlon WebSocket traffic becomes completely untraceable by ISPs and the Relay itself.
